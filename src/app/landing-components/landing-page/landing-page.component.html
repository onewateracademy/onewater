Vision

The Vision of the One Water Academy (OWA) is to develop a One Water workforce for a One Water resilient world.

Mission

The mission of OWA is to provide a One Water knowledge platform to advance resilience through a multi-faceted, state-of-the-art digital learning platform to help accelerate and advance knowledge sharing, education, innovation, and capacity building in the water sector.
